The Beagle Blog

The Beagles is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The Beagles was developed primarily for hunting rabbit or hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the Beagles is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The Beagles is intelligent and popular pet due to its size and good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.

Trivia

  1. Height - 13-16 in (33-41 cm).
  2. Weight - 22-25 lb (10.0-11.3 kg).
  3. Coat -Short haired, hand coat of medium length.
  4. Color -Tricolor or white in combination with black & tan/brown or brown/tan.
  5. Life span - 12-15 years.
